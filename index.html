EDUHERO

EduHero was a website I made for a local tutor who was in need of expanding his clientele. By sitting down and meeting with the client several time I was able to pinpoint exactly how they wanted the website to looks. Everyone from the logo design down to the color scheme. Since the site launched the client has received many new students.

VERTEX

Vertex was a project created by myself and Tyler Nicol while attending Humber College. It is a personal media player that uses Node, The Movie Database’s API, MongoDB, and Vue.js to give users beautiful access to their personal media. It also utilizes a mobile site for gesture control that feels natural.

HEROES ARCHIVE

In the first quarter of 2017 Nintendo and Intelligent Systems published their first mobile game based on the wildly popular franchise “Fire Emblem”. As a fan of the franchise I wanted to create a place where other fans could read up on the information on all the characters, but on a site that looked beautiful. It’s built using Node, MongoDB, and Pug.js. A custom CMS is currently being worked on.

ABOUT

Chris Wilczewski is a web designer and developer who finished the Web Design & Interactive Media program at Humber College in Toronto, Canada. He had been dabbling in web development for several year prior and decided to make the change from I.T. to it. He has a strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, JS, MongoDB, and several Javascript Libraries and Frameworks. When he’s not busy doing work he enjoys expanding his web knowledge, as well as relaxing by playing the guitar, drinking small batch brewed beers, and just hanging out with his friends.
